Featured Books

Dune
by Frank Herbert

A science fiction masterpiece that explores themes of politics, religion, and ecology on the desert planet Arrakis.

The Hobbit
by J.R.R. Tolkien

A fantasy novel about Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit who embarks on an adventure with thirteen dwarves to reclaim their homeland.

1984
by George Orwell

A dystopian novel that explores themes of totalitarianism, surveillance, and the manipulation of truth.

Pride and Prejudice
by Jane Austen

A classic romance novel that follows the emotional development of Elizabeth Bennet as she learns about the repercussions of hasty judgments.

The Great Gatsby
by F. Scott Fitzgerald

A novel that explores the American Dream through the story of Jay Gatsby and his love for Daisy Buchanan.

How to Test

  1. Make sure the Bookscan extension is installed in Firefox
  2. Click the Bookscan extension icon in your toolbar
  3. Enter your OpenAI API key and Hardcover API key in the settings
  4. Click "Scan Page for Books"
  5. The extension should detect the books above and provide options to add them to your reading lists
